Class: isScroll

模拟滚动条

Author: putaoshu@126.com.
View source: isScroll.js.

Arguments:

vui.isScroll (obj, options)

  1. obj - (Object) 主对象
  2. options - (Object) 组件配置
  3. options.top - (Number) null top
  4. options.wrapWidth - (Number) 500 主体宽度
  5. options.wrapHeight - (Number) 200 主体高度
  6. options.wrapClass - (Number) isScrollWrap 主体class
  7. options.scrollWidth - (Number) 20 滚动条宽度
  8. options.scrollMinHeight - (Number) 50 滚动条最小高度
  9. options.scrollClass - (Number) isScroll 滚动条class
  10. options.step - (Number) 5 滚动条步进值
  11. options.setScrollOnce - (Number) false 重新设置滚动条高度Tag

Example: 

vui.isScroll($('#content'));
vui.isScroll($('#content'),{setScrollOnce:true});//重置滚动条
